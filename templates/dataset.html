Dataset Description

The dataset consists of combined sensor data and satellite observations collected to classify soil quality into different categories based on physical, chemical, and environmental properties.

Feature Description
pH Measure of soil acidity or alkalinity affecting nutrient availability.
Temperature (Â°C) Soil temperature influencing microbial activity and plant growth.
Moisture (%) Amount of water present in the soil, impacting root development.
Humidity (%) Atmospheric humidity around the soil surface affecting evaporation rates.
Nitrogen Essential nutrient required for leaf growth and chlorophyll formation.
Phosphorus Nutrient responsible for root development and energy transfer in plants.
Potassium Nutrient that improves disease resistance and overall crop quality.
Rainfall (mm) Amount of precipitation influencing soil moisture and nutrient leaching.
